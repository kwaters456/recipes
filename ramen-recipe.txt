Ingredients:
 - 1 package of your favorite Ramen flavor
 - 1 big dollop of peanut butter


Directions:
 1. Bring a small pot of water to boil
 2. Add Ramen to the water
 3. When the noodles are soft, remove from heat, and drain some of the water.
 4. Add the spice packet and dollop of peanut butter.
 5. Enjoy!
